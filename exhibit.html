TalakBot: Smart Trash Bin with Real-time monitoring via Android Application for Efficient Waste Management

The necessity for effective waste management systems has increased in recent years as a result of the fast expansion in urbanization and population. In this project, TalakBot, a revolutionary smart garbage can with real-time monitoring capabilities through an easy-to-use Android app, is introduced. TalakBot seeks to change waste management procedures by offering more comfort, increased effectiveness, and optimal resource allocation. TalakBot's capacity to continuously and intelligently check the amount of garbage in the bin is its key feature. The smart trash can is equipped with cutting-edge sensors and Internet of Things (IoT) technology, which allows it to identify and quantify the quantity of rubbish present with great accuracy. The Android app serves as a central control point and gives users easy access to manage and keep an eye on several TalakBot devices at once.

Development of an Earthquake Detection System with SMS Notification

This study presents an Arduino-based earthquake detector using an accelerometer, buzzer, and SMS module to provide timely notifications during seismic events. The system detects vibrations exceeding predefined thresholds, activating a buzzer for local alerts and triggering an SMS module for remote notifications. The proposed detector offers a cost-effective and easily deployable solution for earthquake detection, contributing to early warning systems and disaster preparedness.

Design and Development of a Smart Parking System with RFID and Android Interface

The project "Design and Development of a Smart Parking System with RFID and Android Interface" aims to revolutionize parking management by integrating RFID technology and in intuitive Android interface. This project overview provides an introduction to the objectives and purposes of the project, followed by the overview of its main features and functionalities. The project's architecture design and implementation are discussed, highlighting the integration of the RFID technology, the central server, and the android application. Although the desired results are have not been achieved yet, the project emphasizes the importance of efficient pin management and configuring connections to the server and the android application.
